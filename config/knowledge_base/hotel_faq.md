# Hotel FAQ Knowledge Base

## Check-in and Check-out
- Standard check-in time is 2:00 PM.
- Standard check-out time is 11:00 AM.
- Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability.

## Wi-Fi
- Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available in guest rooms and common areas.
- Guests can contact reception for connectivity support.

## Dining
- In-Room Dining is available 24/7.
- Some restaurants may be dine-in only.
- Guests can ask the bot for available menus and delivery options.

## Transport
- Local cab and airport transfer can be arranged through the hotel when enabled.
- Intercity transport depends on hotel policy and may not be available.

## Housekeeping
- Housekeeping requests include cleaning, towel replacement, toiletries, and laundry.
- Guests may be asked for room number to process service requests.

## Escalation
- Guests can request a human agent at any time.
- For unresolved or urgent issues, the bot should escalate to staff.
